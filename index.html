Welcome to InfoMedZ — the unified healthcare platform connecting patients, doctors, nurses, pharmacies, diagnostic labs, hospitals, and clinics across India. This Privacy Policy explains how InfoMedZ Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("InfoMedZ", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application, web platform, and related services (collectively, the "Platform").

By accessing or using InfoMedZ, you agree to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform. This policy applies to all users — patients, healthcare providers, pharmacy operators, and lab technicians.

🏥
Section 01

Overview & Scope

InfoMedZ operates as a regulated healthcare technology platform subject to applicable Indian data protection laws including the Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Amendment) Act, 2008, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), and relevant Ministry of Health guidelines.

This Policy applies to:

  • All registered users — patients, doctors, nurses, physiotherapists, dentists, dieticians
  • Healthcare provider organisations — hospitals, clinics, diagnostic labs, pharmacies, surgical supply shops
  • Visitors who browse the Platform without registering
  • Any person who communicates with InfoMedZ via chat, email, or support channels
HIPAA & Data Sensitivity Notice Medical and health data collected on this Platform is treated as sensitive personal data under Indian law and receives the highest level of protection. We do not sell your health data under any circumstances.

This Policy does not apply to third-party websites linked from the Platform. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies independently.

Section 04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Healthcare Providers: Your medical data is shared with doctors, nurses, pharmacists, or lab technicians only as necessary to provide the specific service you request. Shared data is governed by confidentiality obligations.
  • Service Partners: Payment processors (Razorpay, PayU), cloud infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud), SMS/email providers (Twilio, SendGrid) — all under data processing agreements with strict data protection obligations.
  • Insurance Partners: With your explicit consent, for cashless claims or health insurance integration.
  • Research & Public Health: Only aggregated, de-identified data may be shared with government health authorities (e.g., ICMR, MoHFW) as required by law or for public health surveillance.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by court order, law enforcement request, or to comply with applicable Indian law.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred. We will notify you and allow you to opt out where legally possible.
Explicit Prohibition InfoMedZ strictly prohibits sharing your health or personal data with pharmaceutical companies, advertisers, data brokers, or any commercial third party for marketing purposes without your clear, informed, and revocable consent.

Section 08

Children's Privacy

InfoMedZ is designed for users aged 18 years and above. We understand that children also need healthcare services, and accordingly:

  • Children under 18 may use InfoMedZ only with a parent or legal guardian's account and explicit consent
  • A parent or guardian must create the account and accept this Privacy Policy on behalf of a minor
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13 without verified parental consent
  • Paediatric healthcare data is subject to the same or stricter protections as adult health data
  • Parents or guardians may request access, correction, or deletion of their child's data at any time
Parental Control If you believe your child has provided personal information without your consent, contact us immediately at privacy@infomedz.in and we will promptly delete such data.
